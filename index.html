The phenomenon of the Reformation

In 2017, we celebrated the 500th anniversary of the beginning of a tremendous spiritual movement, which has changed the course of history forever – the Reformation. German philosopher G.W.F. Hegel called the Reformation "a sense of the history of modern times."
The social phenomena, born by the Reformation, is one of the greatest achievements in the history of mankind. Ukrainians also strive towards this phenomena. Western civilization in many ways derives its sources from the spiritual ideals of the Reformation.
The Reformation is the return of individuals, the Church, and society to biblical standards. It is a return to the person of Jesus Christ – the Son of God, the everlasting Word that became flesh. This is the essence, meaning, and power of the Reformation.

Decree of the president of Ukraine.
About the celebration of the 500th
anniversary of Reformation in Ukraine

In recognition of the outstanding contributions of the Protestant churches and religious organizations into the development of religious, cultural and social spheres of Ukraine, the expression of respect for their role in Ukrainian history and formation of the independent state, and also the celebration in Ukraine the 500th anniversary of Reformation, I decree:

  1. 1. To the Cabinet of Ministers:
    • a. develop together with representatives of Protestant religious organizations of Ukraine and in three months approve the plan of events on preparation and celebration of the 500th anniversary of Reformation in Ukraine, providing, inter alia:
      • i. holding the ceremony to mark the 500th anniversary of Reformation in Kyiv in October 2017;
      • ii. conducting thematic conferences, symposia, round tables, seminars and other scientific and educational activities to mark the 500th anniversary of Reformation, develop appropriate guidelines for the enforcement of these events;
      • iii. implementing the training and edition of scientific and popular scientific works dedicated to the 500th anniversary of Reformation, and the collection of works of the Reformation leaders;
      • iv. conducting special events highlighting the history of Reformation in educational institutions;
      • v. preservation and popularization of prominent local landmarks of the spiritual culture;
      • vi. creating museum exhibitions devoted to the 500th anniversary of Reformation;
      • vii. conducting Christian festivals of spiritual music and singing;
      • viii. minting and putting into circulation (in the established order) the coins dedicated to the 500th anniversary of Reformation;
      • ix. releasing of a postage stamp and envelope dedicated to the 500th anniversary of Reformation;
      • x. production and placement of PSAs to mark the 500th anniversary of Reformation;
      • xi. creating and showing documentaries and feature films on the history of the Reformation.
    • b. decide in due course the issue of funding for the preparation and celebration the 500th anniversary of Reformation in Ukraine.
  2. 2. Kyiv City State Administration assists the Protestant religious organizations in conducting the events on the occasion of the 500th anniversary of Reformation in Kyiv in September 2017.
  3. 3. State Committee for Television and Radio Broadcasting of Ukraine in due course provides information about the activities on preparation and celebration of the 500th anniversary of Reformation in the state mass media, creates and broadcasts a series of thematic television and radio programs, demonstrates relevant documentaries and feature films.
  4. 4. The State Security Service Ukraine together with the State Archival Service of Ukraine assist religious and social organizations, academic institutions and organizations, and provide access to the archival documents related to the persecuting members of religious organizations.
  5. 5. To Regional and Kyiv city state administration:
    • a. develop together with regional organizing committees established by Protestant religious organizations and approve the regional action plans to celebrate the 500th anniversary of Reformation, and ensure their implementation;
    • b. hold public celebrations of the 500th anniversary of Reformation involving the representatives of public authorities, local governments and religious organizations;
    • c. assist in the implementation of the initiatives by religious organizations aimed at conduction of educational, cultural, sports and other activities.
  6. 6. This decree takes effect from the date of its publication.

Petro Poroshenko
President of Ukraine
August 26, 2016

Key personalities

IN THE PROGRAM “REFORMATION 500”

Politicians

Due to the participation of important Ukrainian Protestant politicians, the program received huge state support, which in Ukrainian circumstances proved to be extremely crucial. Also, politicians managed to act as a neutral para-denominational factor, which served to unite the various Protestant churches and denominations.

Pavlo Ungurian

Ukrainian politician, public figure, and Member of Parliament of Ukraine of VI and VIII convocations. Deputy Minister of Ecology and Natural Resources of Ukraine (2014). Chairman of the Organising Committee of the Reformation Anniversary Celebration. The success of the Year of Reformation was largely due to his energy and leadership skills.

Oleksandr Turchynov

Is a Ukrainian politician and statesman. Secretary of the National
Security and Defence Council of Ukraine (2014—2019). Member of Parliament of Ukraine. Doctor of Economics, Professor, Member of the Union of Writers of Ukraine (since 2007). From February 22 to November 27, 2014 – Chairman of the Parliament of Ukraine. In the most tragic and turning point of the recent historyof Ukraine, February 23 to June 7, 2014 – Acting President of Ukraine.

Experience of Belarus in 2003

A team of Christians from the EER Ministry initiated the celebration of 450 years of the Reformation in Belarus (1553-2003). Several dozens of different projects were carried out, such as academic, educational, publishing and of an evangelistic nature. The Belarusian Reformation Anniversary brought together all top-level evangelical denominations and spread information about the Reformation in Belarus, as an important part of the national heritage. The project has received strong support from the Belarusian secular intelligentsia. The Ukrainian version, although it was of a much larger scale, widely used the Belarusian experience.

The Reformation Movement

In early 2018, following the completion of the Year of Reformation program, the Organising Committee proposed the creation of the Reformation Movement that would preserve R500 structures and continue its work in certain areas.
The Reformation movement was headed by Pavlo Ungurian, Member of the Parlament of Ukraine. The goals and structure of the Reformation Movement were announced at the Forum on February 23, 2018.

Our relationship with
the Reformation Movement

The Eastern European Reformation Ministry became one of the key participants in the Reformation Movement. Jaroslaw Lukasik became the head of the first department of the RM – namely the Reformation of the Christian leadership Department, which aims at:

  • - formation of holistic biblical worldview and raising the level of knowledge in the sphere of public life of evangelical Christians of Ukraine;
  • - training Christian leaders in the matters of holistic worldview, professionalism and character that have a special calling to spread biblical values in various spheres of public life, such as media, education, social work, politics, administration, etc;
  • - assisting pastors and church ministers in pastoral work with intellectuals and socially active people.
